# WrenchGo Platform Protection Policy

## Overview
WrenchGo requires all job-related communication to remain on the platform to protect both customers and mechanics. This policy explains why this matters, what's allowed, and the consequences of violations.

---

## Why Communication Must Stay On-Platform

### 1. **Payment Protection**
- All payments are secured through WrenchGo's escrow system
- Disputes can only be resolved for on-platform transactions
- Off-platform payments have no protection or recourse

### 2. **Complete Job History**
- Your entire conversation history is preserved
- Essential for warranty claims and repeat bookings
- Helps resolve disputes with documented evidence

### 3. **24/7 Support Access**
- Our support team can only assist with on-platform jobs
- Off-platform issues cannot be investigated or resolved
- Access to mediation and dispute resolution services

### 4. **Trust & Reputation**
- Reviews and ratings build your professional reputation
- Off-platform work doesn't count toward trust scores
- Badges and verification require documented platform activity

### 5. **Easy Rebooking**
- Find your favorite mechanics with one tap
- Preferred mechanics get reduced commission rates
- Automatic job history and preferences

## How We Detect Violations

### Automated Detection
Our system scans messages for:
- Phone numbers (all formats, including obfuscated)
- Email addresses
- Social media handles
- Payment app usernames
- URLs and website links
- Common obfuscation patterns

### Context-Aware Scoring
The system considers:
- **Job Stage**: Stricter during pre-booking, more lenient during active jobs
- **Account History**: New accounts face higher scrutiny
- **User Reputation**: Completed jobs and positive reviews reduce false positives
- **Message Context**: Legitimate patterns (VINs, part numbers) are whitelisted

### False Positive Prevention
We automatically allow:
- Part numbers that resemble phone numbers
- VIN numbers
- Business addresses
- Technical specifications
- Legitimate emergency contact (with job context)

## Progressive Enforcement

### Tier 1: Education (First Offense)
- **Action**: Warning message displayed
- **Impact**: No restrictions, educational only
- **Message**: "Please keep communication on WrenchGo for your protection"
- **Duration**: Warning only, no lasting impact

### Tier 2: Warning (Second Offense)
- **Action**: Formal warning with policy explanation
- **Impact**: Message may be masked or blocked
- **Message**: "Repeated attempts to share contact info violate our policy"
- **Duration**: Warning logged, no account restrictions

### Tier 3: Restriction (Third Offense)
- **Action**: Temporary chat restrictions
- **Impact**: Limited to templated messages only
- **Duration**: 24-48 hours
- **Message**: "Your chat access is temporarily restricted. Use quick reply buttons."

### Tier 4: Review (Fourth+ Offense)
- **Action**: Account review by support team
- **Impact**: Chat suspended pending review
- **Duration**: Until manual review completed
- **Message**: "Your account is under review due to repeated policy violations"

## Legitimate Exceptions

### Emergency Contact (Active Jobs Only)
- **Allowed**: "I'm running late, call if needed: [number]"
- **Context**: Job is in progress, mechanic en route
- **Why**: Safety and coordination during active work

### Warranty Work
- **Allowed**: Coordinating with previous mechanic for warranty issues
- **Context**: Documented previous job on platform
- **Why**: Honoring warranty commitments

### Business Information
- **Allowed**: Shop address, business hours, location details
- **Context**: Any time
- **Why**: Legitimate business information, not circumvention

### Technical Details
- **Allowed**: VINs, part numbers, specifications
- **Context**: Any time
- **Why**: Essential job information, not contact info

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "Why can't I just give my number for emergencies?"
You can! During active jobs, emergency contact is allowed. Our system understands context and won't block legitimate emergency coordination.

### "What if I want to do warranty work for a previous customer?"
Warranty work is allowed! Coordinate through the platform, and our system recognizes legitimate warranty scenarios.

### "I accidentally shared my number. Will I be banned?"
No. First offenses are educational only. We understand mistakes happen and use progressive enforcement.

### "What about sharing my shop address?"
Business addresses are always allowed. Our system distinguishes between contact info and legitimate business information.

### "How do I become a preferred mechanic?"
Simply complete jobs successfully on-platform. The system automatically tracks your relationship with each customer and reduces commissions as you build repeat business.

### "What if the system blocks a legitimate message?"
Contact support immediately. We review all blocks and can whitelist legitimate patterns. False positives are rare but we take them seriously.
